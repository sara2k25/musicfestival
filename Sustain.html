Sustainability


Our Festival, Our Planet.

At Sunny Day Festival, we believe that celebrating together should go hand-in-hand with caring for the environment. Our commitment to sustainability goes beyond awareness — it’s built into the festival’s core.

Here’s how we’re keeping Sunny Day Festival eco-friendly:

  • Reusable Cup and Cutlery System: We’ve eliminated single-use plastics. Purchase a reusable token, exchange it for a cup, return it when you’re done, and get your token back — an easy, clean way to reduce waste.
  • Eco-Zones and Recycling Stations: Clearly marked areas across the festival make it simple to recycle and compost, with volunteers and signage guiding responsible behavior.
  • Green Ambassador Program: Teen volunteers help spread awareness, assist at Eco-Zones, and lead by example. Their efforts are rewarded with free tickets, exclusive access, or recognition.
  • Recycled Art Workshops: Turn trash into treasure by creating collaborative art installations using festival waste — a creative way to learn about upcycling and reuse.
  • Sustainable Energy: Parts of the festival will be powered by solar panels, supporting our goal to reduce carbon emissions and rely on clean, renewable energy where possible.
  • Eco-Friendly Food Vendors: We partner with local and sustainable food suppliers who use biodegradable packaging, offer plant-based menu options, and prioritize organic and locally sourced ingredients.
  • Sunny Hunt App: A digital-only guide and challenge tool — no printed maps or paper guides — which encourages eco-actions like recycling and attending sustainability workshops.

Together, these practices help reduce our environmental impact and inspire attendees to take small, meaningful actions — creating a festival experience that’s joyful, conscious, and future-ready.
