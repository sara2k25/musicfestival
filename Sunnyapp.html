Sunny Hunt App


Discover. Play. Learn. Earn. The Sunny Hunt App is your interactive companion for exploring the festival in a fun, rewarding, and eco-friendly way. It’s more than just a map — it’s your ticket to adventure.

Using the app, attendees can participate in a variety of creative and sustainable challenges. Scan QR codes hidden throughout the festival to collect points for activities such as:

  • Sunny Hunt App: Discover, play, learn, and earn rewards by completing festival challenges.
  • Sustainability Initiatives: Eco-friendly practices, reusable cup systems, recycling stations, and solar-powered energy.
  • Festival Tickets: Affordable options for individuals, families, and groups, including early bird discounts.
  • Creative Workshops: Hands-on activities like painting, pottery, crochet, and cultural storytelling.
  • Adventure & Nature: Ziplining, forest treasure hunts, dinosaur trails, and interactive eco-games.
  • Teen Energy & Family Fun: Social media engagement, festival ambassador program, and interactive app experiences.
  • Sharing photos or videos on Instagram, TikTok, or Facebook using the official hashtag

    • Each completed task earns digital points, which you can track on your profile. Redeem them at the Eco-Shop for exclusive prizes like toys, crafts, merch, or upgrades.


    The app turns the entire festival into a live treasure hunt where learning, creativity, and sustainability all come together in a fun and meaningful way.
