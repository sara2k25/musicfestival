Welcome to Sunny Day Festival!



Sunny Day Festival is a vibrant music festival for families and all ages, set against the breathtaking backdrop of Bran Castle. Blending live performances with creative workshops, outdoor games, and cultural activities, the festival offers something for everyone — from teens and kids to parents and grandparents. Join us for a weekend of music, fun, and unforgettable memories as we come together to shine under the same sun.
