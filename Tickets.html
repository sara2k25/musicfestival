At Sunny Day Festival, we believe that music, creativity, and fun should be accessible to everyone. That’s why we’ve created affordable ticket options for individuals, families, and groups — so no one misses out on the experience.


Ticket Options


  • General Admission – Day Pass: Full access to all music stages, workshops, and activity zones for one day.
    Price: €25 per person
  • General Admission – Weekend Pass (3 Days): Access to the full three-day festival experience.
    Price: €60 per person
  • Family Day Pass: Valid for 2 adults and up to 2 children (under 12 years).
    Price: €60
  • Family Weekend Pass (3 Days): Valid for 2 adults and up to 2 children (under 12 years).
    Price: €140
  • Children under 5: Free admission with a paying adult.
  • Group Discounts: Available for groups of 8 or more. Please contact us for special rates.

Additional Info

  • Early Bird Tickets: A limited number of early bird passes will be available at a further discounted rate. Follow us on social media for updates.
  • Volunteer Passes: Apply to volunteer and enjoy a free day pass in return for helping at the event.
  • Buy Tickets: Tickets will be available on our official website and select local outlets. Secure your spot early!
