Shop

☀ Link to SHOP



The Sunny Day Festival Shop offers a curated selection of merchandise designed to reflect the spirit of the event — joyful, creative, and environmentally conscious.

Our Collection Includes:

  • Eco-friendly apparel: Made from organic cotton and recycled fabrics.
  • Reusable tote bags and water bottles: Designed with our official logo and vibrant festival colors.
  • Handmade crafts and toys: Created from upcycled or biodegradable materials.
  • Limited-edition sustainable souvenirs: Available only at the festival.

You can also redeem your points from the Sunny Hunt App at the Eco-Shop, where both children and teens can choose rewards that are not only fun but planet-friendly.

Whether you're taking home a soft cotton hoodie, a recycled badge, or a nature-themed toy, every item in our shop is chosen with care — for the earth, and for the memories you’ll carry with you.
