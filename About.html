About the Festival



Sunny Day Festival is more than just a music event — it’s a celebration of creativity, connection, and joy for families and people of all ages. Set in the enchanting surroundings of Bran Castle, the festival brings together live music, art, nature, and sustainability in a safe and inspiring space where teenagers, kids, and adults can explore and enjoy together.


Whether you’re dancing to your favorite band, creating a clay masterpiece, or ziplining through the trees, Sunny Day Festival invites you to rediscover the magic of being outside, being present, and shining together.


What Makes Sunny Day Festival Unique?


  • All-Ages Welcome This festival is designed to include everyone — teenagers, children, parents, and even grandparents. Each activity is tailored to encourage participation, learning, and fun across generations.
  • A Diverse Line-Up: From emerging young artists to family-friendly musical acts, the Sunny Day stage brings together a blend of live performances suited to all tastes.
  • Creative Zones: Learn to paint, braid, crochet, or try your hand at pottery in one of our hands-on art workshops. No experience needed — just curiosity and enthusiasm.
  • Adventure & Nature: Set at the foot of Bran Castle, you’ll be surrounded by forests, mountain air, and open space. Enjoy ziplining, forest treasure hunts, dinosaur trails, and nature storytelling adventures for kids and teens.
  • Eco-Friendly & Purpose-Driven: We believe fun and responsibility go hand-in-hand. Sunny Day Festival promotes sustainability, with green initiatives, recycled art zones, and the Sunny Hunt App, which turns eco-awareness into a playful game with real rewards.
  • Family Vibe with Teen Energy: While families are at the heart of the festival, teens are the soul. Everything from the festival ambassador to the interactive app, music selection, and social media integration is designed to inspire and reflect Gen Z’s vibrant, conscious spirit.
  • Festival Experience: Whether you’re coming for the music, the nature, the workshops, or just to spend a day in the sun, Sunny Day Festival welcomes you with open arms and open skies.
